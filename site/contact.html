Suggested Talking Points · AI Consent
01I believe no citizen should be subjected to an AI system without their explicit, informed consent. Please support legislation requiring opt-in consent for AI deployment.
02AI systems are increasingly used in hiring, lending, healthcare, and law enforcement — affecting people's lives without their knowledge. Transparency and consent must be required.
03Children deserve special protection. Any AI system interacting with or analyzing minors should require explicit parental consent and strict data protections.
04Please support the creation of a clear federal right to know when AI is being used to make decisions about you, and the right to opt out.
05The Consent Stack (keepaion.com) is a citizen-led initiative. We are asking representatives to champion an AI Bill of Rights grounded in informed consent.
